Essential Travel Tips

Everything you need to know for an amazing Thailand adventure

Best Time to Visit

November to February offers cool, dry weather perfect for exploring temples and beaches.

Currency

Thai Baht (THB) is the local currency. ATMs are widely available and cards are accepted.

Transportation

Use BTS/MRT in Bangkok, tuk-tuks for short trips, and domestic flights for longer distances.

Culture

Dress modestly at temples, remove shoes when entering homes, and smile often!

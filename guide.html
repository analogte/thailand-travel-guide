Essential Travel Tips

Important information for your Thailand journey

Visa Information

Many nationalities get 30-day visa exemption on arrival. Check requirements for your country before traveling.

Health & Safety

Get travel insurance, drink bottled water, use sunscreen, and be cautious with street food initially.

Internet & SIM

Get a tourist SIM card at the airport. AIS, TrueMove, and DTAC offer affordable data packages.
